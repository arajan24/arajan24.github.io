Swap

An homage to Alfred Hitchcock's noir classic "Strangers on a Train"

Swap

  • This short is a reimagining of the opening scene of Alfred Hitchcock's Strangers on a Train. I love the original film and am so fascinated by the premise. I had the idea of presenting a more modern take on the story written in my own words. As you will see in the film, we tried to give it the visual look of a film from the 50s as well.

  • Written and Directed by Adithya Rajan
  • Cast: Sachit Grover and Adithya Rajan
