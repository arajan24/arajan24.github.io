About Me

My name is Adithya Rajan and I am a filmmaker with a huge passion for directing and producing. I have received my Masters of Fine Arts in Film & TV Directing from DePaul University. Along with directing over 10 short films that have played across festivals around the US, I have experience producing over 15 films & web series. I am very passionate about telling stories that resonate with South Asian Americans across various genres including comedies and thrillers.

I have also graduated with a Bachelors of Science in Electrical Engineering from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I have experience working in product across multiple industries including healthcare, consumer goods, and finance, and have successfully launched multiple projects to market.
