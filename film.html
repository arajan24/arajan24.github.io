Cigarette Daydreams: Dir. Summer Cortes

Cigarette Daydreams is about being stuck between the wedge of honoring and loving oneself despite the pain it might cause another. The pain is not intentional, though it remains inevitable (or so we think). In life, we tend to push for circumstances to be either black or white as there is comfort in boxes and binaries. To love another, and more specifically, choosing to love oneself is never that simple.
